        • Home

          • Facing the dawn of the new century and basking in the spring breeze of curriculum reform, Jiaocheng Middle School in Jiaocheng District, Ningde City, is like a bud emitting a tempting fragrance, slowly blooming at No. 72 Shuyuan Road, Guxi Village, Chengnan Town, Jiaocheng District, Ningde City, Fujian Province. Our school has a strong faculty team, reasonable curriculum construction, and a strong school culture. We adhere to the concept of using the scientific outlook on development to guide our educational practices and create high-quality education that satisfies the people. We warmly welcome guidance from all sectors of society and strive to cultivate new socialist talents.

        • Subjects

          Our school offers a comprehensive curriculum that includes a wide variety of subjects to cater to the diverse interests and talents of our students. We have biology, which delves into the intricacies of life and living organisms; Chinese, to preserve and appreciate our cultural heritage; math, to hone our logical and analytical skills; PE, to keep us physically fit and active; geography, to broaden our understanding of the world and its environments; art, to nurture our creative spirits; history, to learn from the past and understand the present; English, to communicate effectively on a global scale; Music, to foster our appreciation of the arts and enhance our aesthetic senses; and computer science, to equip us with digital literacy and prepare us for the future technological landscape.

        • Students and Teachers

          The relationship between students and teachers at our Jiaocheng Middle School is very good, fostering an environment of mutual respect, trust, and support. This positive dynamic not only encourages academic excellence but also promotes personal growth and development. Parents, observing the harmonious interactions between educators and learners, feel at ease knowing that their children are in a nurturing and encouraging environment. The open lines of communication and collaborative efforts between students and teachers create a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels valued and heard, contributing to a sense of community and belonging among all stakeholders.

        • Clubs

          There are numerous clubs and extracurricular activities available at our Jiaocheng Middle School, catering to a wide array of interests and passions. For instance, we have a vibrant sports club that organizes various athletic events and training sessions, fostering teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness among its members. Additionally, there's a dedicated running club that encourages students to embrace the joys of jogging and marathon training, instilling a sense of perseverance and self-challenge. These clubs not only provide opportunities for students to engage in physical activities but also serve as platforms for socializing, making friends, and developing leadership skills. By participating in these clubs, students can explore their interests, discover new hobbies, and build a sense of belonging within the school community. Furthermore, the clubs contribute to the holistic development of students, helping them balance their academic and extracurricular pursuits effectively.
